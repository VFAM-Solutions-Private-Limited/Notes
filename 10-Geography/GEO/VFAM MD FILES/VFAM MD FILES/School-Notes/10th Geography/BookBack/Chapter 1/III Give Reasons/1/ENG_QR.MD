### Q1: Why are Himalayas called **Young Fold Mountains**?
> **3 Key Points to Remember:**
> 1. Formed only **few million years ago** → **YOUNG**
> 2. Formed by **folding of Earth's crust** → **FOLD**
> 3. Eurasian + Gondwana land masses pushed together → Tethys Sea folded up = **HIMALAYAS**
 
**Memory Trick:** *"Young people FOLD clothes"*
