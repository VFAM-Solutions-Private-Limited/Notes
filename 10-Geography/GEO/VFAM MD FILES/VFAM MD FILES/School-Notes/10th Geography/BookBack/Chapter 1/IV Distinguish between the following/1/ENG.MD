### Himalayan Rivers and Peninsular Rivers

| Sr. No. | Himalayan Rivers | Peninsular Rivers |
|---------|------------------|-------------------|
| 1. | Originate from the Himalayas | Originate from the Western Ghats |
| 2. | Long and wide | Short and narrow |
| 3. | Perennial in nature | Non-Perennial in nature |
| 4. | Unsuitable for hydropower generation | Suitable for hydropower generation |
| 5. | Middle and lower courses are navigable | Not useful for navigation |
| 6. | **Examples:** Indus, Ganga, Brahmaputra | **Examples:** Mahanadi, Godavari, Krishna, Cauvery, Narmada, Tapti |
