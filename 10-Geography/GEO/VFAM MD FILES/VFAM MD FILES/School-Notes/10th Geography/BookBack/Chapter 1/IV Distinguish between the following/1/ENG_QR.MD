### Himalayan Rivers vs Peninsular Rivers
 
| Point | **Himalayan** | **Peninsular** |
|---|---|---|
| Water | Perennial ✔ | Seasonal ✗ |
| Size | Long & Wide | Short & Narrow |
| Source | Glaciers + Rain | Only Rain |
| Power | Less hydro power | **More hydro power** |
| Navigation | **Navigable** ✔ | Not navigable ✗ |
| Examples | Ganga, Indus | Godavari, Kaveri |
