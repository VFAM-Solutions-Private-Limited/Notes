### Himalayan Rivers vs Peninsular Rivers
 
| **Himalayan** | **Peninsular** |
|---|---|
| Perennial ✔ | Seasonal ✗ |
| Long & Wide | Short & Narrow |
| Glaciers + Rain | Only Rain |
| Less hydro power | **More hydro power** |
| **Navigable** ✔ | Not navigable ✗ |
| Ganga, Indus, Brahmaputra | Godavari, Krishna, Cauvery, Narmada, Tapti |
