### 2. Western Ghats vs Eastern Ghats
 
| Feature | **Western Ghats** | **Eastern Ghats** |
|---|---|---|
| **Location** | Western edge of Deccan Plateau | Eastern edge of Deccan Plateau |
| **Direction** | Runs parallel to Arabian Sea coast | Runs from SW to NE |
| **Continuity** | **Continuous** range | **Discontinuous** (broken) range |
| **Rainfall** | Receives heavy rainfall | Receives less rainfall |
| **Height** | Higher in south (Anaimudi – 2695m) | Lower than Western Ghats |
| **Rivers** | Source of many peninsular rivers | Dissected by rivers |
| **Other name** | Sahyadri (northern part) | Poorvadri |
| **Meeting point** | Meet at Nilgiri Hills | Meet at Nilgiri Hills |
