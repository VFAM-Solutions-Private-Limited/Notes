### 3. Western Coastal Plains vs Eastern Coastal Plains
 
| Feature | **Western Coastal Plains** | **Eastern Coastal Plains** |
|---|---|---|
| **Location** | Between Western Ghats and Arabian Sea | Between Eastern Ghats and Bay of Bengal |
| **Width** | Narrow (10–80 km) | Wider than western coastal plains |
| **Shape** | Irregular shoreline | Regular shoreline |
| **Features** | Lagoons, backwaters (Kayals), sand dunes | Well-defined beaches, deltas |
| **Famous lakes** | Vembanad Lake | Chilka Lake, Kolleru Lake, Pulicat Lake |
| **Parts** | Konkan, Kanara, Malabar coast | Northern Circars, Coromandal coast |
| **States** | Gujarat to Kerala | West Bengal to Tamil Nadu |
