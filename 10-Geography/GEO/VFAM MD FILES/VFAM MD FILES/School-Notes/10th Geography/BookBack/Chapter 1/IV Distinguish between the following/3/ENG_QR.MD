###  Western Coastal Plains vs Eastern Coastal Plains
 
| Point | **Western** | **Eastern** |
|---|---|---|
| Sea | Arabian Sea | Bay of Bengal |
| Width | Narrow | Wider |
| Famous lake | **Vembanad** | **Chilka Lake** |
| Parts | Konkan, Kanara, Malabar | Circars, Coromandal |
| Shoreline | Irregular | **Regular** |
