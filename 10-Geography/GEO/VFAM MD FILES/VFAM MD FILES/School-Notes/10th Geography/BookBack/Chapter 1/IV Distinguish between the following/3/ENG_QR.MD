### 3.  Western Coastal Plains vs Eastern Coastal Plains

| **Western Coastal Plains** | **Eastern Coastal Plains** |
|---|---|
| Arabian Sea | Bay of Bengal |
| Rann of Kutch → Kanyakumari | West Bengal → Tamil Nadu |
| Sandy Beaches, Lagoons, Estuary | Alluvial Deposits |
| Konkan, Kanara, Malabar | Northern Circars, Coromandel |
| No Deltas | Forms Deltas |
| Narrow (10–80 km) | Wide (80–100 km) |
