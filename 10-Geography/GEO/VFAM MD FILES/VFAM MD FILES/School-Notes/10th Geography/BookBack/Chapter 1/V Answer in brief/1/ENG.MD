### 1. Name the Neighbouring Countries of India
 
India shares its borders with **7 countries**:
 
- **North-West**: Pakistan and Afghanistan
- **North**: China, Nepal, and Bhutan
- **East**: Bangladesh and Myanmar
- **South** (across sea): Sri Lanka (separated by Palk Strait)
> **Longest border** – with **Bangladesh** (4097 km)
> **Shortest border** – with **Afghanistan** (106 km)
