### 2. Importance of **Indian Standard Time (IST)**
 
- India extends from **Gujarat (west) to Arunachal Pradesh (east)** — a difference of about **30 degrees longitude**.
- Because of this, Arunachal Pradesh gets **sunrise 2 hours earlier** than Gujarat.
- If each state used its own local time, it would cause **confusion** in railways, communication, and offices.
- To avoid this, India uses **one common standard time** called **IST**.
- IST is based on **82°30' E longitude** (passing through **Mirzapur**, Uttar Pradesh).
- IST is **5 hours 30 minutes ahead** of Greenwich Mean Time (GMT).
