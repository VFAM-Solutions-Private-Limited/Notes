### 2. Importance of IST
- India has **30° longitude difference** (Gujarat to Arunachal Pradesh)
- Arunachal Pradesh gets **sunrise 2 hours earlier** than Gujarat
- **IST = 82°30'E** longitude (passes through **Mirzapur**)
- **IST = GMT + 5 hours 30 minutes**
- One standard time → **No confusion** in transport, offices, communication
