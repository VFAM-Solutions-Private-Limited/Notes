### 3. Short Note on **Deccan Plateau**

- The **Deccan Plateau** is the **largest part of the Peninsular Plateau**.
- It is **roughly triangular** in shape.
- One side extends from **Kanyakumari to Rajmahal Hills** through the **Eastern Ghats**.
- The second side is formed by the **Satpura Range, Mahadeo Hills, Maikal Range and Rajmahal Hills**.
- The third side is marked by the **Western Ghats**.
- Area is about **7 lakh sq.km** and height ranges from **500 to 1000 m** above sea level.
- The plateau slopes **from west to east**, so most rivers flow eastward.
- Major rivers: **Godavari, Krishna, Kaveri and Mahanadi** — all drain into the **Bay of Bengal**.
