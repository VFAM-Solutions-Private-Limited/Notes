### 3. Short Note on **Deccan Plateau**
 
- The **Deccan Plateau** is the largest part of the Peninsular Plateau.
- It is **roughly triangular** in shape.
- It lies **south of the Narmada River**.
- **Boundaries**: Western Ghats (west), Eastern Ghats (east), Satpura + Vindhya Range (north)
- Area is about **7 lakh sq.km** and height ranges from **500 to 1000 m** above sea level.
- The plateau slopes **from west to east** — so rivers flow eastward.
- Major rivers: **Godavari, Krishna, Kaveri, Mahanadi** — all flow into the Bay of Bengal.
- It is formed from the ancient **Gondwana land mass**.
