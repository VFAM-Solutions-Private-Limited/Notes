### Deccan Plateau (Quick Recall)

> - Largest Peninsular Plateau
> - Roughly triangular
> - Kanyakumari → Rajmahal Hills (Eastern Ghats)
> - Satpura + Mahadeo + Maikal + Rajmahal Hills
> - Western Ghats
> - Area: **7 lakh sq.km**
> - Height: **500–1000 m**
> - Slopes **West → East**
> - Rivers: **Godavari, Krishna, Kaveri, Mahanadi**
