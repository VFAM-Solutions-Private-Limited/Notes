### 4. West Flowing Rivers of India
 
Only a **few rivers** in Peninsular India flow towards the west:
 
| River | Origin | Empties Into |
|---|---|---|
| **Narmada** | Amarkantak Plateau, MP | Arabian Sea 
| **Tapti (Tapi)** | Betul district, MP | Arabian Sea
| **Mahi** | Madhya Pradesh | Arabian Sea |
| **Sabarmati** | Rajasthan | Arabian Sea |
