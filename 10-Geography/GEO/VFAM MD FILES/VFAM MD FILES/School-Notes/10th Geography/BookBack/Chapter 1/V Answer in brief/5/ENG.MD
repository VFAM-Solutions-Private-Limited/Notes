### 5. Brief Note on **Lakshadweep Islands**
 
- Lakshadweep is a group of **coral islands** located off the **west coast of India** in the **Arabian Sea**.
- It has **27 islands** covering an area of only **32 sq.km**.
- Administrative capital: **Kavaratti**
- It is separated from the **Maldive Islands** by the **Eight Degree Channel**.
- Earlier it had three parts: **Laccadive, Minicoy, and Amindivi** — renamed **Lakshadweep in 1973**.
- The uninhabited **Pitt Island** has a **bird sanctuary**.
- The islands are made of **coral** (not volcanic).
