### 1. Northern Mountains — Divisions & Importance
 
**3 MAIN DIVISIONS:**
 
**① Trans-Himalayas** → North of Himalayas → Ranges: Zaskar, Ladakh, Kailash, Karakoram
 
**② The Himalayas** → 3 sub-ranges:
- **Greater Himalayas (Himadri)** → Height: 6000m → Everest (8848m), Kanchenjunga (8586m)
- **Lesser Himalayas (Himachal)** → Hill stations: Shimla, Mussoorie, Nainital, Darjeeling
- **Outer Himalayas (Siwaliks)** → Height: 900–1100m → Duns (west), Duars (east)
**③ Purvanchal Hills** → North-East India → Naga, Khasi, Garo, Manipur Hills
 
**IMPORTANCE — 6 POINTS:**
1. **Blocks cold winds** from Central Asia
2. **Causes rainfall** — stops monsoon winds
3. **Natural defence barrier** for India
4. **Source of perennial rivers** (Ganga, Indus, Brahmaputra)
5. **Tourist paradise + Pilgrimage centres** (Amarnath, Kedarnath, Badrinath)
6. **Rich biodiversity**
