### 2. Major Peninsular Rivers
 
**EAST FLOWING (flow into Bay of Bengal):**
 
| River | Origin | Length | Special Fact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mahanadi** | Sihawa, Chhattisgarh | 851 km | Large delta |
| **Godavari** | Nasik, Maharashtra | 1465 km | Longest peninsular river; "Vridha Ganga" |
| **Krishna** | Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra | 1400 km | 2nd longest peninsular river |
| **Kaveri** | Kodagu, Karnataka | 800 km | "Dakshina Ganga" |
