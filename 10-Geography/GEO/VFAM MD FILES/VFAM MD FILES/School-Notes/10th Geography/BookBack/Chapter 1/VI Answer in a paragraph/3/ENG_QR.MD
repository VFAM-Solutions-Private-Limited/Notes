### 3. Ganga River Basin — Key Points
 
| Topic | Details |
|---|---|
| **Origin** | Gangotri Glacier, Uttarkhand (as **Bhagirathi**) |
| **Height of origin** | 7010 m |
| **Length** | 2525 km |
| **Drainage area** | 8,61,404 sq.km (**largest in India**) |
| **Largest tributary** | **Yamuna** (from south) |
| **Other tributaries** | Gomti, Ghaghra, Gandak, Kosi (north); Son, Chambal (south) |
| **Name in Bangladesh** | **Padma** |
| **Delta** | **Sundarbans** — World's largest delta |
| **Empties into** | Bay of Bengal |
