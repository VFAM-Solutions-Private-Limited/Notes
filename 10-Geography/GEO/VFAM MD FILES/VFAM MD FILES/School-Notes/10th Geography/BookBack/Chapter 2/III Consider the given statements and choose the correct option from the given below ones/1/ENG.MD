## **III. Assertion and Reason**
 
### 1. Assertion (A): The Himalayas acts as a **climatic barrier**.
### Reason (R): The Himalayas prevents **cold winds from Central Asia** and keeps the Indian Sub-continent warm.
 
**Answer: a) Both (A) and (R) are true: R explains A**
 
**Explanation:**
- The Himalayas truly acts as a **climatic barrier** — this is the Assertion, and it is **TRUE**.
- The Reason is also **TRUE** — the Himalayas blocks freezing cold winds coming from Central Asia.
- Because of this blocking, North India remains warm even during winter and enjoys **tropical climate**.
- The Reason **correctly explains** why the Himalayas is a climatic barrier.
- So, the correct answer is **(a)**.
