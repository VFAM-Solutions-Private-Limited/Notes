### 1. Factors Affecting the **Climate of India**
 
The climate of India is affected by the following **6 factors**:
 
1. **Latitude** — Position of a place north or south of the equator
2. **Altitude** — Height of a place above sea level
3. **Distance from the Sea** — How far a place is from the ocean
4. **Monsoon Wind** — Seasonal winds that bring rain
5. **Relief Features** — Mountains, plains, plateaus etc.
6. **Jet Streams** — Fast-moving winds in the upper atmosphere
