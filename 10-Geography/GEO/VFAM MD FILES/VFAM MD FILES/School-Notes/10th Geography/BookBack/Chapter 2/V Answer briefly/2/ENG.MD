### 2. What is **Normal Lapse Rate**?
 
- When we go up (increase altitude), the **temperature decreases**.
- The temperature decreases at the rate of **6.5°C for every 1000 metres of height**.
- This fixed rate of temperature decrease with height is called the **Normal Lapse Rate**.
- **Example:** If Chennai (at 6.7m) is 35°C, then Ooty (at 2240m) will be much cooler because of the normal lapse rate.
