### 3. What are **Jet Streams**?
 
- **Jet streams** are **very fast moving winds** that blow in a **narrow zone** in the **upper atmosphere**.
- According to the **Jet Stream Theory**:
  - The shift of the **sub-tropical westerly jet** from the Indian plains towards the **Tibetan Plateau** causes the onset of the **Southwest Monsoon**.
  - The **easterly jet streams** cause **tropical depressions** (low pressure zones) during both the Southwest Monsoon and the retreating monsoon season.
- Jet streams also play a major role in bringing **Western Disturbances** to India during winter.
