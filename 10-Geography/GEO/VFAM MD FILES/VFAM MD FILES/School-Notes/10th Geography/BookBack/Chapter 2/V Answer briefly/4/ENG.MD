### 4. Short Note on **Monsoon Wind**
 
- The word **"Monsoon"** comes from the **Arabic word "Mausim"** meaning **"season"**.
- It was first used by **Arab navigators** to describe **seasonal reversal winds** over the Indian Ocean and Arabian Sea.
- **During summer**: Monsoon blows from **Southwest to Northeast**.
- **During winter**: Monsoon blows from **Northeast to Southwest**.
- Monsoon winds are the **most dominant factor** affecting India's climate.
- About **75% of India's rainfall** comes from the **Southwest Monsoon**.
- According to the **Dynamic Concept**, monsoon originates due to the **seasonal migration of planetary winds and pressure belts** following the position of the sun.
