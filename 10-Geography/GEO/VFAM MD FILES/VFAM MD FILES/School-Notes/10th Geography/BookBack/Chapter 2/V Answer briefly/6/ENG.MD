### 6. What is **Burst of Monsoon**?
 
- Before the Southwest Monsoon arrives, the temperature in North India reaches up to **46°C**.
- When the monsoon wind suddenly arrives over **South India** with **lightning and thunder**, it is called the **"Burst of Monsoon"** or **"Break of Monsoon"**.
- It happens during the **first week of June** at the southern tip of India.
- The sudden arrival brings down temperatures sharply and causes **heavy rainfall**.
