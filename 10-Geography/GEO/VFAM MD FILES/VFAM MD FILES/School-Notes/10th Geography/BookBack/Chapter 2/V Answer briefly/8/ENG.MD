### 8. Places of **Mangrove Forests** in India
 
Mangrove forests (also called **delta/swamp forests**) are found in:
- **Sundarbans** — in the Ganga-Brahmaputra Delta (largest tidal forest in the world)
- **Mahanadi Delta** (Odisha)
- **Godavari Delta** (Andhra Pradesh)
- **Krishna Delta** (Andhra Pradesh)
