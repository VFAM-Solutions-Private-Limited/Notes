### 9. Any **Five Biosphere Reserves** of India
 
India has **18 Biosphere Reserves** in total. Five important ones are:
 
| # | Biosphere Reserve | State |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Nilgiris** | Tamil Nadu / Kerala / Karnataka |
| 2 | **Sundarbans** | West Bengal |
| 3 | **Gulf of Mannar** | Tamil Nadu |
| 4 | **Nanda Devi** | Uttarakhand |
| 5 | **Pachmarhi** | Madhya Pradesh |
 
> **11 out of 18** Biosphere Reserves in India are listed under **UNESCO's Man and Biosphere Programme**.
