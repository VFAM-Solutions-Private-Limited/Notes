### 2. Tropical Evergreen Forest vs Tropical Deciduous Forest
 
| Feature | **Tropical Evergreen Forest** | **Tropical Deciduous Forest** |
|---|---|---|
| **Rainfall** | More than **200 cm** | **100 to 200 cm** |
| **Temperature** | Above **22°C** | About **27°C** |
| **Humidity** | Above **70%** | **60–70%** |
| **Leaf shedding** | **Do NOT shed** leaves — stay green all year | **Shed leaves** during spring and early summer |
| **Other name** | — | **Monsoon Forests** |
| **Key trees** | Rubber, Mahogany, Ebony, Rosewood | **Teak and Sal** (most important) |
| **Location** | Western Ghats, Andaman & Nicobar, Assam | Sub-Himalayan region, Central and South India |
