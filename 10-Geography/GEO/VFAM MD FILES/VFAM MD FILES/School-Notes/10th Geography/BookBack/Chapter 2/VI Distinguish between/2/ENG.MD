### Tropical Evergreen Forests and Deciduous Forests
 
| **Tropical Evergreen Forests** | **Deciduous Forests** |
|---|---|
| These forests are found in the areas where the annual rainfall is 200 cm and more. | These forests are found in the areas with 100 to 200 cm annual rainfall. |
| The trees of these forests do not shed their leaves. | The trees of these forests shed their leaves in spring and early summer. |
| Rubber, Iron wood and Mahogany are some of the main trees. | Sandalwood, Teak, Sal and Padak are some of the main trees. |
| **Examples:** Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Western Ghats and North Eastern States. | **Examples:** Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha and parts of Tamil Nadu. |
