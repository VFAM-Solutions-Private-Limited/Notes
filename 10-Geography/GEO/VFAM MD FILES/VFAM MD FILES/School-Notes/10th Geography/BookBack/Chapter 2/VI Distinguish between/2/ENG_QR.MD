### Tropical Evergreen vs Tropical Deciduous Forest
 
| Point | **Evergreen** | **Deciduous** |
|---|---|---|
| Rainfall | **>200 cm** | **100–200 cm** |
| Leaves | **Don't shed** | **Shed in spring** |
| Other name | — | **Monsoon Forest** |
| Key trees | Rubber, Mahogany, Ebony | **Teak and Sal** |
| Humidity | >70% | 60–70% |
