### North East Monsoon and South West Monsoon
 
| **North East Monsoon** | **South West Monsoon** |
|---|---|
| These monsoon winds blow from **North-east to South-west**. | These monsoon winds blow from **South-west towards North-east**. |
| They blow during the months of **October-November**. | They blow during the months of **June-September**. |
| **Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh** get rainfall from this monsoon. | **75% of India's rainfall** is from these monsoon winds. |
| It is also known as the **Retreating Monsoon**. | It is also known as the **Advancing Monsoon**. |
