### 3. Northeast Monsoon vs Southwest Monsoon
 
| Feature | **Southwest Monsoon** | **Northeast Monsoon** |
|---|---|---|
| **Season** | June – September (Rainy Season) | October – December |
| **Direction** | Blows from **Southwest to Northeast** | Blows from **Northeast to Southwest** |
| **Rainfall %** | About **75%** of India's annual rainfall | About **35%** for South India |
| **Areas affected** | Most of India | Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, South Karnataka |
| **Origin** | ITCZ shifts north → SE trade winds deflect | Pressure belts shift south → NE trade winds form |
| **Branches** | Arabian Sea branch + Bay of Bengal branch | No branches — single wind system |
| **Special feature** | "Burst of Monsoon" at onset | Causes **cyclones** in Bay of Bengal |
