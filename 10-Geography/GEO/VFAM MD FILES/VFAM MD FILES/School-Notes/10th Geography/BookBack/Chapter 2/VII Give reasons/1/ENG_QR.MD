### India has a Monsoon Type of Climate
 
> **5 Keywords = Monsoon Climate**
>
> - **20°N – 20°S Region** → Monsoon belt
> - **Season Changes** → Wind direction changes
> - **Indian Ocean** → Source of monsoon winds
> - **SW Winds** → April to October
> - **NE Winds** → October to April
 
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Monsoon = Seasonal Wind Reversal**
>
> **SW (Apr–Oct) → NE (Oct–Apr)**
