### 1. India has **Tropical Monsoon Climate** — Because:
1. Located mostly in **tropical zone** (8°N to 37°N)
2. **Tropic of Cancer** passes through middle — southern half is tropical
3. **Monsoon winds** dominate Indian climate
4. **Himalayas** blocks cold winds → keeps India warm
5. **3 seas** around — Indian Ocean, Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal → moisture for rain
6. Clear **4 seasonal cycles** driven by monsoon
