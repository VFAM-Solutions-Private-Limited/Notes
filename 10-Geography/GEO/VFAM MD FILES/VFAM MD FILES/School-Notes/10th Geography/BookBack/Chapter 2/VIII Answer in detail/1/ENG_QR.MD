### 1. Southwest Monsoon — Key Points
 
**Onset Timeline:**
- **1st week of June** → Arrives at Kerala (southern tip)
- **Early June** → Advances along Konkan coast
- **15th July** → Covers entire India
**2 Branches:**
 
| Branch | Route | Special Fact |
|---|---|---|
| **Arabian Sea** | Hits Western Ghats → North India | Rajasthan gets less rain (Aravalli parallel to wind) |
| **Bay of Bengal** | NE India → Myanmar → blocked by Khasi/Garo/Jaintia hills | **Mawsynram** gets world's highest rain (1141 cm) |
 
**Rainfall east to west:** Decreases as Bay of Bengal branch moves west.
