### 1. Can You Imagine a World Without Agriculture?
 
No, it is almost impossible to imagine a world without agriculture. Here is why:
 
- **No food:** Without farming, there would be no rice, wheat, vegetables, fruits, or any food. Humans would have nothing to eat.
- **No clothing:** Cotton and jute, which give us cloth and bags, come from agriculture. We would have no natural fabric.
- **No industries:** Sugar factories, cotton mills, jute factories, tea companies, and many other industries depend on agricultural raw materials. All these would shut down.
- **Mass unemployment:** More than 50% of Indians work in agriculture. Without it, hundreds of millions of people would have no work.
- **No animals:** Animals like cows and buffaloes are fed on crop waste. Without farming, they would also starve.
- **Back to primitive life:** Before farming was invented (about 10,000 years ago), humans were hunter-gatherers who had no permanent homes and were always searching for food. We would go back to that kind of difficult life.
Agriculture is not just a job or a business. It is the very **foundation of human civilization**. Without it, modern society cannot exist.
