### 2. Solutions for Water Disputes in South India
 
- **Rainwater Harvesting** - collect local rain, reduce river dependence
- **Drip Irrigation** - save 70% water, less need for river water
- **Interlinking rivers** - share water from surplus to deficit areas
- **Permanent Tribunal** - strong legal body to enforce decisions
- **Change crops** - shift from water-heavy crops (paddy) to millets/pulses
- **Wastewater recycling** - treat and reuse industrial/city water
- **Inter-state Water Authority** - all states manage river together
