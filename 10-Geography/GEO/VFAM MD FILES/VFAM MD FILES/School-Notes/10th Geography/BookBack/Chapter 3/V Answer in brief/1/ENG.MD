### 1. Define Soil
 
**Soil** is the **topmost layer of the Earth's surface**. It is made up of:
- **Minerals** (from broken rocks)
- **Organic matter** (from dead plants and animals)
- **Living organisms** (like earthworms and bacteria)
- **Air**
- **Water**
Soil is formed slowly when rocks break down due to sun, rain, and wind. The tiny particles in soil are of three types: **clay, silt, and sand**.
