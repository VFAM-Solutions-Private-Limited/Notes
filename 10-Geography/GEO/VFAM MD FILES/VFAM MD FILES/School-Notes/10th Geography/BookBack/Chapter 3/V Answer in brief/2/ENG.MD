### 2. Types of Soil Found in India
 
The **Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)**, set up in 1953, divided Indian soils into **8 major types**:
 
1. **Alluvial Soil**
2. **Black Soil**
3. **Red Soil**
4. **Laterite Soil**
5. **Forest and Mountain Soil**
6. **Arid and Desert Soil**
7. **Saline and Alkaline Soil**
8. **Peaty and Marshy Soil**
