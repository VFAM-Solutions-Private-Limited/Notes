### 4. Define Agriculture
 
**Agriculture** is the process of:
- **Cultivating plants** to produce food for people and fodder (food) for animals
- **Raising animals (livestock)** for milk, meat, wool, etc.
- Also producing **fibre** (like cotton and jute) and other products
In simple words, agriculture means **farming** - growing crops and raising animals for our needs.
