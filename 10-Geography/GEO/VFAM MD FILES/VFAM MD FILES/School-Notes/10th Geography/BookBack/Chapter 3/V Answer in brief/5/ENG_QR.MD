### 5. Types of Agriculture (6 Types)
 
| Type | Key Point |
|---|---|
| **Subsistence** | Grow for family, sell little surplus |
| **Shifting** | Clear forest, farm 2-3 yrs, move (Slash & Burn) |
| **Intensive** | Max output from small land using machines + chemicals |
| **Dry Farming** | No irrigation, drought-resistant crops only |
| **Mixed Farming** | Crops + Animals + Poultry + Fishery together |
| **Terrace Farming** | Hill slopes cut into steps to grow crops |
