### 6. Agriculture Seasons in India
 
India has **3 main cropping seasons**:
 
| Season | Months | Examples of Crops |
|---|---|---|
| **Kharif** | June to September | Rice, Cotton, Maize, Bajra, Jowar |
| **Rabi** | October to March | Wheat, Gram, Mustard, Barley |
| **Zaid** | April to June | Vegetables, Fruits, Fodder crops |
