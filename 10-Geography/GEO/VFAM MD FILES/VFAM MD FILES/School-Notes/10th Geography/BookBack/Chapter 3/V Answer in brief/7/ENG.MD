### 7. Plantation Crops of India
 
The **4 main plantation crops** of India are:
 
1. **Tea** - Grown mainly in Assam, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, West Bengal
2. **Coffee** - Grown mainly in Karnataka (71% of India's production)
3. **Rubber** - First planted in Kerala in 1902; needs hot and wet climate
4. **Spices** - Pepper, chilli, cardamom, ginger, turmeric etc.; Kerala is the leading producer
These crops are grown on **large estates on hilly slopes** and are mainly cultivated for **export**.
