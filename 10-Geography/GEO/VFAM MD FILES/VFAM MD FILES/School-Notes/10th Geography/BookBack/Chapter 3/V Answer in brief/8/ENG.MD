### 8. Categories of Fisheries in India
 
India's fishing industry is divided into **2 main categories**:
 
**1. Marine (Sea) Fisheries:**
- Fishing done in the **sea** - coastal areas, offshore and deep sea
- Includes fishing on the **continental shelf**
- **Kerala** is the leading state in marine fish production
- Important fish: Mackerel, Herrings, Mullets, Eels
**2. Inland (Fresh Water) Fisheries:**
- Fishing done in **rivers, lakes, canals, ponds, tanks, and reservoirs**
- Contributes about **50% of total fish production** in India
- **Andhra Pradesh** is the leading state in inland fishing
- Important fish: Cat fish, Perches
India produces about **3% of the world's fish** and ranks **2nd in the world** after China.
