### 8. Fisheries - 2 Categories
 
| Category | Where | Leading State |
|---|---|---|
| **Marine (Sea)** | Ocean / Sea | **Kerala** |
| **Inland (Fresh water)** | Rivers, lakes, ponds | **Andhra Pradesh** |
 
- India = **2nd in world** fish production (after China)
- Inland fisheries = **50%** of total fish production
