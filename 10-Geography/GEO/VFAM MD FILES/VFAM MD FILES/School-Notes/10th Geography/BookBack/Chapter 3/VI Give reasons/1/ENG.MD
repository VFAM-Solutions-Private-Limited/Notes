### 1. Agriculture is the Backbone of India
 
Agriculture is called the backbone of India for the following reasons:
 
- **Food supply:** Agriculture provides food for India's huge population of over 140 crore people.
- **Employment:** More than half of India's population works in agriculture for their livelihood.
- **Raw materials:** Industries like cotton, sugar, jute, and tea get their raw materials from agriculture.
- **Export earnings:** Agricultural products like rice, tea, spices, and cotton earn **foreign exchange** for India.
- **Animal support:** Agriculture also includes livestock rearing which provides milk, meat, wool, and draught power.
- **Rural economy:** Most of India's villages depend completely on agriculture for their income.
Without agriculture, India's economy would completely collapse. That is why it is rightly called the **backbone of India**.
