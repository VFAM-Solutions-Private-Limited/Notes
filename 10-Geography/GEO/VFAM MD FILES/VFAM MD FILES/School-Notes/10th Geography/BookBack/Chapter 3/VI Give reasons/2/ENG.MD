### 2. Rainwater Harvesting is Necessary
 
Rainwater harvesting (collecting and storing rainwater) is necessary because:
 
- **Irregular rainfall:** India receives rain only during the monsoon season (June to September). For the rest of the year, water is scarce.
- **Uneven distribution:** Some areas get too much rain while others get very little. Storing rain during wet months helps in dry months.
- **Falling groundwater levels:** Too much pumping of groundwater (borewells) is depleting underground water. Harvesting rain recharges this water.
- **Water disputes:** States often fight over river water. Harvesting local rain reduces dependence on rivers.
- **Agriculture needs:** Crops need water throughout the year. Stored rainwater helps farmers during dry spells.
- **Drinking water:** In many villages and towns, collected rainwater is an important source of drinking water.
