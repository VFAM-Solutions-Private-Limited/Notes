### Rabi and Kharif Crops
 
| **Rabi** | **Kharif** |
|---|---|
| Rabi crops are sown at the beginning of the winter (i.e.) in **November**. | Kharif crops are sown at the beginning of the monsoon (i.e.) in **June**. |
| The crops are harvested at the beginning of summer (i.e.) in **March**. | The crops are harvested in the early days of **November**. |
| Major crops are **wheat, tobacco, mustard, pulses, linseed and grains**. | Major crops are **paddy, maize, cotton, millets, jute and sugarcane**. |
| These are known as **winter crops**. | These are known as **monsoon crops**. |
