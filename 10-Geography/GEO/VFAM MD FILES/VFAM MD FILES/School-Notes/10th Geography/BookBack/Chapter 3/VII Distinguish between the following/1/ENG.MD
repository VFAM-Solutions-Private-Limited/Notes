### 1. Rabi and Kharif Crop Seasons
 
| Point | **Rabi Season** | **Kharif Season** |
|---|---|---|
| **Time period** | October to March | June to September |
| **Sowing time** | Sown in October-November | Sown in June-July |
| **Harvesting time** | Harvested in March-April | Harvested in September-October |
| **Season** | Winter crops | Summer/monsoon crops |
| **Water source** | Depend on irrigation | Depend on monsoon rains |
| **Main crops** | Wheat, Barley, Mustard, Gram | Rice, Cotton, Maize, Bajra, Jowar |
| **Leading states** | Punjab, Haryana, UP | West Bengal, Punjab, UP |
