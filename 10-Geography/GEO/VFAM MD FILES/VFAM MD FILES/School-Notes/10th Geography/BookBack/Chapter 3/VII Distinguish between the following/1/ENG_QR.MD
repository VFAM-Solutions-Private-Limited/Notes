### Rabi vs Kharif
 
| **Rabi** | **Kharif** |
|---|---|
| **November** sowing | **June** sowing |
| **March** harvest | **November** harvest |
| Winter crops | Monsoon crops |
| Wheat, Tobacco, Mustard | Paddy, Cotton, Maize |
| Cool season | Rainy season |
 
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Rabi = Winter → Nov to Mar**
>
> **Kharif = Monsoon → Jun to Nov**
