### 2. Inundation Canal and Perennial Canal
 
| Point | **Inundation Canal** | **Perennial Canal** |
|---|---|---|
| **Water source** | Directly from river, no dam or barrage built | From perennial rivers after building a barrage or dam |
| **When available** | Only during rainy/flood season | Available throughout the year |
| **Purpose** | To divert flood water from rivers | To regulate and supply water all year |
| **Reliability** | Not reliable - depends on floods | Very reliable |
| **Infrastructure** | No barrage or dam needed | Barrage or dam is required |
| **Example** | Old canals of North India | Indira Gandhi Canal, Bhakra Canal |
