### Inundational Canal vs Perennial Canal
 
| **Inundational Canal** | **Perennial Canal** |
|---|---|
| No barrage or dam | Barrage or dam present |
| Not useful for irrigation | Useful for irrigation |
| Sutlej river canals in Punjab | Found in Punjab, Haryana and U.P. |
| Water flows only during floods | Water flows throughout the year |
 
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Inundational = Flood Season Only**
>
> **Perennial = Water All Year**
