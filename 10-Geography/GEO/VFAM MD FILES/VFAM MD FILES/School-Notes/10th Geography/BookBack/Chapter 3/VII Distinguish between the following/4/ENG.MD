### Alluvial Soils and Black Soils
 
| **Alluvial Soils** | **Black Soils** |
|---|---|
| Alluvial soils are formed by the sediments deposited by the rivers and streams. | Black soils are formed by the disintegration of basalt rocks. |
| These soils are silt clay, sandy and loamy in nature. | These soils are sticky when wet and develop cracks when dry. |
| Alluvial soils are found in the river valleys and plains. | Black soils are found in plateau regions especially in the Deccan Trap. |
| Rice, wheat and sugarcane grow well in this soil. | Cotton, sugarcane and tobacco grow well in this soil. |
