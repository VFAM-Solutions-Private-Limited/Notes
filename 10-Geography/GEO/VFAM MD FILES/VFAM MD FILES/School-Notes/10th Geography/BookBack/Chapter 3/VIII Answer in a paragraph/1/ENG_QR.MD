### 1. Five Soil Types - Quick Summary
 
| Soil | Formed From | Key Feature | Crops | Location |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Alluvial** | River deposits | Most fertile | Rice, Wheat | Ganga plains |
| **Black** | Basalt rocks | Holds water, cracks when dry | Cotton | Maharashtra |
| **Red** | Old granite rocks | Red due to iron | Wheat, Rice | Deccan east, TN |
| **Laterite** | Leaching process | Acidic, poor in nutrients | Coffee, Rubber | Assam hills, Kerala |
| **Forest/Mountain** | Mechanical weathering | Rich in humus, acidic | Tea, Potato | J&K, Himachal |
