### 3. Intensive vs Mixed Farming - Key Points
 
| Point | **Intensive Farming** | **Mixed Farming** |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | Maximum yield from small land | Multiple income sources |
| Uses | Machines, chemicals, HYV seeds | Crops + animals + poultry + fish |
| Risk | High (single crop failure = big loss) | Low (many activities) |
| Effect on soil | Can damage over time | More sustainable |
