### 4. Rice vs Wheat - Geographical Conditions
 
| Factor | **Rice** | **Wheat** |
|---|---|---|
| Temperature | **24°C** (hot) | **10-15°C** sowing, **20-25°C** ripening |
| Rainfall | **150 cm+** (very high) | **75-100 cm** (moderate) |
| Soil | Clayey / loamy, water retaining | Alluvial, well-drained loamy |
| Season | Kharif (Monsoon) | Rabi (Winter) |
| Labour | Needs a lot | Needs less |
| Leading state | **West Bengal** | **Uttar Pradesh** |
