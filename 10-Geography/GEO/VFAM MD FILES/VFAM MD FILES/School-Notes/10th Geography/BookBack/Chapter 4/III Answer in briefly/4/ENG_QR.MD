### 4. What is Natural Gas?

> **Natural Gas = Methane + Petroleum + Fossil Fuel**
>
> - Mainly consists of **Methane**
> - Found along with **Petroleum**
> - Formed from **decomposed plants and animals**
> - Produced by **heat and pressure over thousands of years**
>
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **MPHP → Methane, Petroleum, Heat, Pressure**
