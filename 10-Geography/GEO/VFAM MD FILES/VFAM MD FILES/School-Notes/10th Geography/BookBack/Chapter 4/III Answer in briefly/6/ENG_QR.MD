### 6. Mention the Major Areas of Jute Production in India.

> **Major Jute Producing Areas:**
>
> - **West Bengal** → Along the **Hoogly River** near **Kolkata**
> - Other states:
>   - Andhra Pradesh
>   - Bihar
>   - Uttar Pradesh
>   - Assam
>   - Chhattisgarh
>   - Odisha
>
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **WB + Hoogly = Main Jute Belt**
>
> **ABUACO → Andhra, Bihar, UP, Assam, Chhattisgarh, Odisha**
