### 1. Renewable Resources vs Non-renewable Resources

| **Renewable Resources** | **Non-renewable Resources** |
|---|---|
| Renewed in a short time | Takes millions of years to form |
| Renewal time varies | Formed over long geological periods |
| Solar, Wind, Water, Forests, Wildlife | Coal, Petroleum |

> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Renewable = Reused Quickly**
>
> **Non-renewable = Takes Millions of Years**
