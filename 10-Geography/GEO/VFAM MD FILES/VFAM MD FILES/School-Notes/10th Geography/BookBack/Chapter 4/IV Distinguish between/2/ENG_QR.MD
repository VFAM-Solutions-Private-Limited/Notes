### 2. Metallic Minerals vs Non-metallic Minerals

| **Metallic Minerals** | **Non-metallic Minerals** |
|---|---|
| Contain metals | Do not contain metals |
| Ferrous and Non-ferrous | Organic and Inorganic |
| Good conductors | Poor conductors |
| Iron, Copper, Gold | Coal, Petroleum, Mica |

> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Metallic = Metals + Good Conductors**
>
> **Non-metallic = No Metals + Poor Conductors**
