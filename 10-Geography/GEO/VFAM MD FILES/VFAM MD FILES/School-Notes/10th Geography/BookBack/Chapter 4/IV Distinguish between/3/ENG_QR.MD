### 3. Agro-based Industry vs Mineral-based Industry

| **Agro-based Industry** | **Mineral-based Industry** |
|---|---|
| Uses agricultural products | Uses minerals |
| Cotton, Jute, Sugar industries | Iron and Steel industry |
| Located near cultivation areas | Located near coalfields or iron ore mines |

> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Agro = Agriculture**
>
> **Mineral = Mines**
