### 4. Jute Industry vs Sugar Industry

| **Jute Industry** | **Sugar Industry** |
|---|---|
| Produces gunny bags, canvas, carpets, twines | Produces sugar from sugar cane and sugar beet |
| Main centres: West Bengal, Titagarh, Jagatdal | Major producers: UP, Maharashtra, Karnataka, TN |
| Second important textile industry | Second largest agro-based industry |

> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Jute = Bags and Textiles**
>
> **Sugar = Sugarcane and Agro Industry**
