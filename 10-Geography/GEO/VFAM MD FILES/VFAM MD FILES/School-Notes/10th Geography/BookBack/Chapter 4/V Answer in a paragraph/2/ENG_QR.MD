### 2. Describe the major challenges of Indian industries.

> **Major Challenges of Indian Industries:**
>
> - Power shortage and fluctuation
> - Lack of large land areas
> - Poor access to credit
> - High interest rates on loans
> - Shortage of cheap labour
> - Lack of technical and vocational training
> - Poor living conditions near industrial estates
>
> **Memory Trick:**
>
> **Power → Land → Credit → Interest → Labour → Training → Living Conditions**
