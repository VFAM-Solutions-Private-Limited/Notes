### 3. Write a note on Pipeline network transport in India.

- Pipelines are a very convenient mode of transport to connect oil and natural gas fields, refineries and markets.
- They can be laid through difficult terrain as well as under water.
- They ensure a steady supply of goods and reduce transhipment loss and delays.

**Three important networks of pipeline are:**

1. Oil fields in Upper Assam and Kanpur.
2. From Salaya in Gujarat to Jalandhar in Punjab.
3. From Hazira in Gujarat to Jagadispur in Uttar Pradesh.
