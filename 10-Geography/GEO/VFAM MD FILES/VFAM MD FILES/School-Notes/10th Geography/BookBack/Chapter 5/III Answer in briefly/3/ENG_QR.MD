### 3. Write a note on Pipeline network transport in India.

> **Pipeline Transport:**
>
> - Connects oil fields, natural gas fields, refineries and markets
> - Can pass through difficult terrain and under water
> - Ensures steady supply and reduces losses
>
> **Important Pipeline Networks:**
>
> 1. Upper Assam → Kanpur
> 2. Salaya (Gujarat) → Jalandhar (Punjab)
> 3. Hazira (Gujarat) → Jagadispur (Uttar Pradesh)
