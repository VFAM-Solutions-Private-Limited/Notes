### 4. State the major Inland waterways of India.

The major inland waterways of India are:

1. **National Waterway I:** It extends between **Haldia and Allahabad**, measures **1620 km** and includes the stretches of the **Ganga – Bhagirathi – Hooghly river system**.

2. **National Waterway II:** This waterway includes the stretch of the **Brahmaputra river** between **Dhubri and Sadiya** covering a distance of **891 km**.

3. **National Waterway III:** This waterway extends between **Kollam and Kottappuram** in the state of **Kerala**. It is the first national waterway in the country with **24-hour navigation facilities** along its entire stretch of **205 km**.
