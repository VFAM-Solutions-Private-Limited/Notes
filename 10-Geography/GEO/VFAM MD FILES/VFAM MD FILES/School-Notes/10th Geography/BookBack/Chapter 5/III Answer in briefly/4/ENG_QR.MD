### 4. State the major Inland waterways of India.

> **Major Inland Waterways of India:**
>
> - **National Waterway I** → Haldia to Allahabad → 1620 km → Ganga–Bhagirathi–Hooghly
> - **National Waterway II** → Dhubri to Sadiya → 891 km → Brahmaputra
> - **National Waterway III** → Kollam to Kottappuram → 205 km → Kerala
