### 5. What is communication? What are its types?

> **Communication = Exchange of information, thoughts and ideas**
>
> **Types:**
>
> - Personal Communication
>   - Post and Telegraph
>   - Telephone
>   - Mobile Phone
>   - SMS
>   - Fax
>   - Internet
>   - E-mail
>
> - Mass Communication
>   - Radio
>   - Television
>   - Internet
>   - Newspapers
>
