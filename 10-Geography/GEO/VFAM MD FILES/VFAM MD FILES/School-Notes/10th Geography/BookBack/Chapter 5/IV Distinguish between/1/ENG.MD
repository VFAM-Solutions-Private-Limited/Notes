### 1. Personal communication and mass communication.

| **Personal communication** | **Mass communication** |
|---|---|
| Exchange of communication between the individuals. | Millions of people get the information at the same time. |
| Enables the user to establish direct contact. | Provide information through print media and electronic media. |
| It includes post and telegraph, telephones, mobile phones, short message service (SMS), fax, Internet, e-mail, etc. | It includes Radio, Television and the Internet. Electronic media, newspapers, magazines, books, journals, etc. |
