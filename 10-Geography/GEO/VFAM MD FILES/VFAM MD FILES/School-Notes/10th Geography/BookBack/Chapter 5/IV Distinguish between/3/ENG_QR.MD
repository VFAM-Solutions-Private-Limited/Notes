### 3. Roadways and Railways.

| **Roadways** | **Railways** |
|---|---|
| Short-distance transport | Long-distance transport |
| Cheap and easy to construct | Costly and terrain dependent |
| Highways and roads | Broad, Meter, Narrow and Light gauge |
| Second longest road network | Largest railway network in Asia |
| Door-to-door service possible | No door-to-door service |
