### 4. Waterways and Airways.

| **Waterways** | **Airways** |
|---|---|
| Oldest and cheapest means of transport. | Modern and costliest means of transport. |
| Most suitable for carrying heavy and bulky materials. | Only limited weight can be carried. Cannot carry heavy and bulky materials. |
| Two types: Inland waterways (rivers, backwaters and canals) and oceanic routes connecting coastal areas. | Domestic air service operates within the country and international airways connect major cities of the world. |
