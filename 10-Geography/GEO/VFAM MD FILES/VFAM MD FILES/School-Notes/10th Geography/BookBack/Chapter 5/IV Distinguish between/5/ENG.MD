### 5. Internal trade and International trade.

| **Internal trade** | **International trade** |
|---|---|
| Trade is carried on within the domestic territory. | Trade is carried on between two or more countries. |
| Land transport plays a major role (Roadways and Railways). | Waterways and Airways play a vital role. |
| The local currency is used. | Foreign currency is involved. |
| Helps to promote balanced regional growth. | Helps to promote the country's economy and raise the standard of living. |
