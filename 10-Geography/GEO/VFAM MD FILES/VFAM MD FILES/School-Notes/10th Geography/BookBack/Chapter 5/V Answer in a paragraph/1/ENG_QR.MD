### 1. What is urbanization? Explain its problem.

> **Urbanization = Transformation from rural to urban society**
>
> **Indicators:**
>
> - Population size of towns and cities
> - Population engaged in non-agricultural sectors
>
> **Impacts:**
>
> - Related to economic development
> - Increasing rapidly in developing countries
> - Rural to urban migration causes population explosion
>
> **Problems:**
>
> - Urban sprawl
> - Overcrowding
> - Housing shortage
> - Slums
> - Traffic congestion
> - Water scarcity
> - Drainage problems
> - Solid waste management issues
> - Increase in crime rate
>
