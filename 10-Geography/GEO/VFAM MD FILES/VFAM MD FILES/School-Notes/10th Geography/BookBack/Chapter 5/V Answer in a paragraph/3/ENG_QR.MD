### 3. Classify and explain the roadways in India.

> **Indian Road Network**
>
> - Cost-efficient and popular mode of transport
> - Second longest road network in the world
>
> **Types of Roadways:**
>
> 1. Village Roads → Villages ↔ Towns
> 2. District Roads → Towns ↔ District Headquarters
> 3. State Highways → State Capitals ↔ District Headquarters
> 4. National Highways → State Capitals ↔ National Capital
> 5. Golden Quadrilateral → Major cities of India
> 6. Expressways → Six lane high speed roads
> 7. Border Roads → Northern and Northeastern borders
> 8. International Highways → India ↔ Neighbouring countries
>
