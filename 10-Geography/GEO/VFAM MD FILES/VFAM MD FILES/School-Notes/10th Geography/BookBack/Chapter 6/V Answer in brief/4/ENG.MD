### 4. Name the major islands of Tamil Nadu.

The major islands of Tamil Nadu are:

- Pamban Island
- Hare Island
- Krusadai Island
- Nallathanni Theevu
- Pullivasal Island
- Srirangam Island
- Upputanni Island
- Island Grounds
- Kattupalli Island
- Quibble Island
- Vivekananda Rock Memorial
