# Exercise 1.4

## Question 3:

Represent the function

```text
f = {(1,2), (2,2), (3,2), (4,3), (5,4)}
```

through

```text
(i) An arrow diagram

(ii) A table form

(iii) A graph
```
---

## Solution:

#### Given:

```text
f = {(1,2), (2,2), (3,2), (4,3), (5,4)}
```

The ordered pairs are:

```text
(1,2)

(2,2)

(3,2)

(4,3)

(5,4)
```

The domain is

```text
A = {1,2,3,4,5}
```

The codomain is

```text
B = {2,3,4}
```
