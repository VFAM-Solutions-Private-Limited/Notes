# Example 1.5

## Question:

The arrow diagram shows (Fig. 1.10) a relationship between the sets **P** and **Q**.

  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jeevanya25Ravi/Vfam_Roteen/main/Roteen%20Maths/Example%201.5.png" width="250">
</p>

Write the relation in

```text
(i) Set builder form

(ii) Roster form

(iii) Find the domain and range of R.
```

---

## Solution:

#### Given:

```text
P = {5,6,7,8}

Q = {3,4,5}
```

From the arrow diagram,

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jeevanya25Ravi/Vfam_Roteen/main/Roteen%20Maths/Example%201.5.png" width="250">
</p>

Hence,

### (i) Set Builder Form

```text
R = {(x,y) | y = x − 2, x ∈ P, y ∈ Q }
```

---

### (ii) Roster Form

```text
R = {(5,3), (6,4), (7,5)}
```

---

### (iii) Domain and Range

**Domain**

```text
Domain(R)

= {5,6,7}
```

**Range**

```text
Range(R)

= {3,4,5}
```

---

## Final Answer:

```text
(i) R = {(x,y) ∈ P × Q | y = x − 2}

(ii) R = {(5,3), (6,4), (7,5)}

(iii) Domain(R) = {5,6,7}

      Range(R) = {3,4,5}
```
