# Example 1.9

## Question:

Given

```text
f(x) = 2x − x²
```

find

```text
(i)  f(1)

(ii) f(x+1)

(iii) f(x) + f(1)
```

---

## Solution:

#### Given:

```text
f(x) = 2x − x²
```

### (i) Find f(1)

Substitute \(x=1\):

```text
f(1)

= 2(1) − (1)²

= 2 − 1

= 1
```

---

### (ii) Find f(x+1)

Substitute \(x=x+1\):

```text
f(x+1)

= 2(x+1) − (x+1)²

= 2x + 2 − (x² + 2x + 1)

= 2x + 2 − x² − 2x − 1

= 1 − x²
```

---

### (iii) Find f(x) + f(1)

```text
f(x) + f(1)

= (2x − x²) + 1

= 2x − x² + 1
```

---

## Final Answer:

```text
(i)  f(1) = 1

(ii) f(x+1) = 1 − x²

(iii) f(x) + f(1) = 2x − x² + 1
```
