# Exercise 1.3

## Question 3:

Given the function f: x → x²-5x+6, evaluate:

(i) f(-1)<br>
(ii) f(2a)<br>
(iii) f(2)<br>
(iv) f(x-1)

---
## Solution:

Given:

```text
f(x) = x² - 5x + 6
```

### (i) f(-1)

```text
f(-1) = (-1)² - 5(-1) + 6
      = 1 + 5 + 6
      = 12
```

```text
f(-1) = 12
```

---

### (ii) f(2a)

```text
f(2a) = (2a)² - 5(2a) + 6
      = 4a² - 10a + 6
```

```text
f(2a) = 4a² - 10a + 6
```

---

### (iii) f(2)

```text
f(2) = (2)² - 5(2) + 6
     = 4 - 10 + 6
     = 0
```

```text
f(2) = 0
```

---

### (iv) f(x - 1)

```text
f(x - 1) = (x - 1)² - 5(x - 1) + 6
```

Expand and simplify:

```text
= x² - 2x + 1 - 5x + 5 + 6
= x² - 7x + 12
```

```text
f(x - 1) = x² - 7x + 12
```

---

## Final Answer:

```text
(i)  f(-1)  = 12

(ii) f(2a)  = 4a² - 10a + 6

(iii) f(2)  = 0

(iv) f(x-1) = x² - 7x + 12
```
