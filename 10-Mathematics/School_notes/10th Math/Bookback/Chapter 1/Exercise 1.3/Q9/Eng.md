# Exercise 1.3

## Question 9:

A plane is flying at a speed of 500 km per hour.

Express the distance d travelled by the plane as a function of time t in hours.

---

## Procedure to solve (For reference):

1. Note the speed of the plane.
2. Use the formula:

```text
Distance = Speed × Time
```

3. Substitute the given speed.
4. Express the distance d as a function of time t.

---

## Solution:

Given:

```text
Speed of the plane = 500 km/h
```

We know that,

```text
Speed = distance
        ────────
          time

Distance = speed x time
```

Substituting the given speed:

```text
d = 500 × t
```

Therefore,

```text
d(t) = 500t km
```

---

## Final Answer:

```text
d(t) = 500t km
```

where

```text
t ≥ 0
```

and d is measured in kilometres.
