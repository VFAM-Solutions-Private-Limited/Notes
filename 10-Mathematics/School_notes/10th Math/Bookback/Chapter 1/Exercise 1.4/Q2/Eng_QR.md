# Exercise 1.4

## Question 2:

Let f : A →  B be a function defined by

```text
          x
f(x) =   ─── - 1
          2
```

where

```text
A = {2, 4, 6, 10, 12}

B = {0, 1, 2, 4, 5, 9}
```

Represent f by

```text
(i) Set of ordered pairs

(ii) A table

(iii) An arrow diagram

(iv) A graph
```

---

## Solution:

#### Given:

```text
          x
f(x) =   ─── - 1
          2

A = {2, 4, 6, 10, 12}

B = {0, 1, 2, 4, 5, 9}
```

Calculating the values of \(f(x)\):

```text
        2
f(2) = ─── - 1
        2

     = 1 - 1

     = 0
```

```text
        4
f(4) = ─── - 1
        2

     = 2 - 1

     = 1
```

```text
        6
f(6) = ─── - 1
        2

     = 3 - 1

     = 2
```

```text
        10
f(10) = ──── - 1
         2

      = 5 - 1

      = 4
```

```text
        12
f(12) = ──── - 1
         2

      = 6 - 1

      = 5
```

---

#### (i) Set of Ordered Pairs

```text
f = {(2,0), (4,1), (6,2), (10,4), (12,5)}
```

---

#### (ii) Table

| x | 2 | 4 | 6 | 10 | 12 |
|---|---|---|---|----|----|
| f(x) | 0 | 1 | 2 | 4 | 5 |
