

# Exercise 6.1

## Question 2:

```text
Prove the following identities.

          1 - tan²θ
(i)      ───────────── = tan²θ
          cot²θ - 1


           cosθ
(ii)     ───────── = secθ - tanθ
         1 + sinθ
```

---


## Solution

#### (i)

```text
          1 - tan²θ
        ───────────── = tan²θ
          cot²θ - 1
```


```text
        1 - tan²θ
LHS = ─────────────
        cot²θ - 1

        1 - tan²θ
    = ─────────────    (∵ cotθ = 1/tanθ    ∵ cot²θ = 1/tan²θ)
          1                
        ────── - 1
        tan²θ
     

        1 - tan²θ
    = ─────────────
        1 - tan²θ
        ───────────
          tan²θ

    = (1 - tan²θ) × tan²θ
      ────────────────────
            1 - tan²θ

    = tan²θ

    = RHS

LHS = RHS

          1 - tan²θ
        ───────────── = tan²θ
          cot²θ - 1

Hence Proved.
```

---

## (ii)

```text
         cosθ
       ───────── = secθ - tanθ
        1 + sinθ
```

### Solution

```text
          cosθ
LHS = ─────────
       1 + sinθ

        cosθ(1 - sinθ)
   = ─────────────────────
     (1 + sinθ)(1 - sinθ)

        cosθ(1 - sinθ)
   = ─────────────────────    (∵ (a+b)(a-b)=a²-b²)
           1 - sin²θ
       

        cosθ(1 - sinθ)
   = ─────────────────────   (∵ 1 - sin²θ = cos²θ)
            cos²θ
    

      1 - sinθ
   = ───────────
        cosθ

      1      sinθ
   = ──── - ──────
     cosθ   cosθ

   = secθ - tanθ    (∵ 1/cosθ = secθ, sinθ/cosθ = tanθ)
       
    

   = RHS

LHS = RHS

         cosθ
       ───────── = secθ - tanθ
        1 + sinθ

Hence Proved.
```
