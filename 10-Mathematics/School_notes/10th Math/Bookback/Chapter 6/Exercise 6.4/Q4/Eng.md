# Exercise 6.4

## Question 4:

The angle of elevation of the top of a cell phone tower from the foot of a high apartment is 60° and the angle of depression of the foot of the tower from the top of the apartment is 30°. If the height of the apartment is 50 m, find the height of the cell phone tower. According to radiations control norms, the minimum height of a cell phone tower should be 120 m. State if the height of the above mentioned cell phone tower meets the radiation norms.

## Procedure:

1. Let the height of the apartment be 50 m.
2. Find the horizontal distance between the apartment and the tower using the angle of depression 30°.
3. Use the angle of elevation 60° to find the difference in heights between the tower and the apartment.
4. Determine the height of the tower.
5. Compare the result with the minimum required height of 120 m.

---

## Solution:

Let

- $AB = 50$ m be the height of the apartment.
- $CD = h$ m be the height of the cell phone tower.
- $BC = x$ m be the horizontal distance between the apartment and the tower.

Since the angle of depression from the top of the apartment to the foot of the tower is $30^\circ$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tan 30^\circ
&= \frac{AB}{BC}
\\
\frac{1}{1.732}
&= \frac{50}{x}
\\
x
&= 50 \times 1.732
\\
&= 86.60
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
BC = 86.60\text{ m}
$$

Since the angle of elevation of the top of the tower from the foot of the apartment is $60^\circ$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tan 60^\circ
&= \frac{CD}{BC}
\\
1.732
&= \frac{h}{86.60}
\\
h
&= 86.60 \times 1.732
\\
&= 149.99
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
h \approx 150
$$

Therefore, the height of the cell phone tower is

$$
\boxed{150\text{ m}}
$$

### Verification of Radiation Norms

Minimum required height of a cell phone tower

$$
120\text{ m}
$$

Height of the given tower

$$
150\text{ m}
$$

Since

$$
150 > 120
$$

the height of the cell phone tower satisfies the radiation control norms.

### Answer

- Height of the cell phone tower:

$$
\boxed{150\text{ m}}
$$

- The tower **meets the radiation control norms**.
