# Exercise 6.4

## Question 5:

The angles of elevation and depression of the top and bottom of a lamp post from the top of a 66 m high apartment are 60° and 30° respectively. Find

(i) The height of the lamp post.

(ii) The difference between height of the lamp post and the apartment.

(iii) The distance between the lamp post and the apartment.

$(\sqrt{3}=1.732)$

## Procedure:

1. Let the height of the apartment be 66 m.
2. Use the angle of depression $30^\circ$ to find the horizontal distance between the apartment and the lamp post.
3. Use the angle of elevation $60^\circ$ to find the difference in heights between the lamp post and the apartment.
4. Find the height of the lamp post.
5. Find the required difference in heights.
6. State the horizontal distance between the apartment and the lamp post.

---

## Solution:

Let

- $AB = 66$ m be the height of the apartment.
- $CD = h$ m be the height of the lamp post.
- $BC = x$ m be the horizontal distance between the apartment and the lamp post.

### Step 1: Distance between the apartment and the lamp post

Since the angle of depression of the foot of the lamp post is $30^\circ$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tan 30^\circ
&= \frac{AB}{BC}
\\
\frac{1}{1.732}
&= \frac{66}{x}
\\
x
&= 66 \times 1.732
\\
&= 114.312
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
BC = 114.312\text{ m}
$$

### Step 2: Difference in heights

Since the angle of elevation of the top of the lamp post is $60^\circ$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\tan 60^\circ
&= \frac{CD-AB}{BC}
\\
1.732
&= \frac{h-66}{114.312}
\\
h-66
&= 114.312 \times 1.732
\\
&= 197.99
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
h-66 = 197.99\text{ m}
$$

### Step 3: Height of the lamp post

$$
\begin{aligned}
h
&= 66 + 197.99
\\
&= 263.99
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
h \approx 264
$$

### Answers

#### (i) Height of the lamp post

$$
\boxed{264\text{ m}}
$$

#### (ii) Difference between the heights of the lamp post and the apartment

$$
\boxed{198\text{ m}}
$$

#### (iii) Distance between the lamp post and the apartment

$$
\boxed{114.31\text{ m}}
$$
