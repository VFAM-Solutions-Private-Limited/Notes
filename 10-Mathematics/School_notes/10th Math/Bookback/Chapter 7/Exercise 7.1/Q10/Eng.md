# Exercise 7.1

## Question 10:

The frustum shaped outer portion of the table lamp has to be painted including the top part. Find the total cost of painting the lamp if the cost of painting 1 sq.cm is ₹ 2.

## Procedure:

1. Identify the dimensions of the frustum.
2. Find the slant height of the frustum.
3. Calculate the curved surface area of the frustum.
4. Calculate the area of the top circular surface.
5. Find the total area to be painted.
6. Multiply the area by the cost per sq.cm.

---

## Solution:

From the figure,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Upper diameter}
&= 6\text{ cm}
\\
\text{Lower diameter}
&= 12\text{ cm}
\\
\text{Height}
&= 8\text{ cm}
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
\begin{aligned}
r
&=\frac{6}{2}
\\
&=3\text{ cm}
\\
R
&=\frac{12}{2}
\\
&=6\text{ cm}
\\
h
&=8\text{ cm}
\end{aligned}
$$

Slant height of the frustum,

$$
\begin{aligned}
l
&=\sqrt{h^2+(R-r)^2}
\\
&=\sqrt{8^2+(6-3)^2}
\\
&=\sqrt{64+9}
\\
&=\sqrt{73}
\end{aligned}
$$

Curved surface area of the frustum,

$$
\begin{aligned}
CSA
&=\pi(R+r)l
\\
&=\frac{22}{7}(6+3)\sqrt{73}
\\
&=\frac{198\sqrt{73}}{7}
\\
&\approx241.71\text{ cm}^2
\end{aligned}
$$

Area of the top circular surface,

$$
\begin{aligned}
A
&=\pi r^2
\\
&=\frac{22}{7}\times3^2
\\
&=\frac{198}{7}
\\
&\approx28.29\text{ cm}^2
\end{aligned}
$$

Total area to be painted,

$$
\begin{aligned}
T
&=CSA+A
\\
&=241.71+28.29
\\
&=270\text{ cm}^2
\end{aligned}
$$

Cost of painting,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Cost}
&=270\times2
\\
&=540
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
\boxed{\text{Total cost of painting}=₹540}
$$
