# Exercise 7.1

## Question 1:

The radius and height of a cylinder are in the ratio 5:7 and its curved surface area is 5500 sq.cm. Find its radius and height.

---
## Solution:

Given,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Radius : Height}
&= 5:7
\\
\text{Curved Surface Area}
&= 5500\text{ cm}^2
\end{aligned}
$$

Let

$$
\begin{aligned}
r &= 5x
\\
h &= 7x
\end{aligned}
$$

Curved surface area of a cylinder,

$$
\text{CSA}=2\pi rh
$$

Substituting the values,

$$
\begin{aligned}
5500
&= 2\times\frac{22}{7}\times(5x)\times(7x)
\\
&= 44\times5x^2
\\
&= 220x^2
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
x^2
&= \frac{5500}{220}
\\
&= 25
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
x=5
$$

Radius:

$$
\begin{aligned}
r
&= 5x
\\
&= 5(5)
\\
&= 25\text{ cm}
\end{aligned}
$$

Height:

$$
\begin{aligned}
h
&= 7x
\\
&= 7(5)
\\
&= 35\text{ cm}
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
\boxed{\text{Radius}=25\text{ cm}}
$$

$$
\boxed{\text{Height}=35\text{ cm}}
$$
