# Exercise 7.1

## Question 5:

4 persons live in a conical tent whose slant height is 19 m. If each person require 22 m² of the floor area, then find the height of the tent.

---

## Procedure:

1. Find the total floor area required for 4 persons.
2. Use the area of the circular base of the tent to find its radius.
3. Apply the relation between radius, height and slant height of a cone.
4. Find the height of the tent.

---

## Solution:

Given,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Number of persons}
&= 4
\\
\text{Floor area required per person}
&= 22\text{ m}^2
\\
\text{Slant height }(l)
&= 19\text{ m}
\end{aligned}
$$

Total floor area required,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Floor area}
&= 4 \times 22
\\
&= 88\text{ m}^2
\end{aligned}
$$

Since the floor of the tent is circular,

$$
\pi r^2 = 88
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{22}{7}r^2
&= 88
\\
r^2
&= \frac{88\times7}{22}
\\
&= 28
\end{aligned}
$$

For a cone,

$$
l^2 = r^2 + h^2
$$

Substituting the values,

$$
\begin{aligned}
19^2
&= 28 + h^2
\\
361
&= 28 + h^2
\\
h^2
&= 333
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
h
&= \sqrt{333}
\\
&\approx 18.25\text{ m}
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
\boxed{\text{Height of the tent }=18.25\text{ m}}
$$
