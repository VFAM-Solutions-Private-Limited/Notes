# Exercise 7.2

## Question 4:

A conical container is fully filled with petrol. The radius is 10 m and the height is 15 m. If the container can release the petrol through its bottom at the rate of 25 cu. meter per minute, in how many minutes the container will be emptied. Round off your answer to the nearest minute.

---

## Solution:

Given,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Radius }(r)
&= 10 \text{ m}
\\
\text{Height }(h)
&= 15 \text{ m}
\\
\text{Rate of flow}
&= 25 \text{ m}^3/\text{min}
\end{aligned}
$$

Volume of the conical container,

$$
\begin{aligned}
V
&= \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h
\\
&= \frac{1}{3}\times\frac{22}{7}\times10^2\times15
\\
&= \frac{22}{7}\times500
\\
&= \frac{11000}{7}
\\
&= 1571.43 \text{ m}^3
\end{aligned}
$$

Time taken to empty the container,

$$
\begin{aligned}
t
&= \frac{\text{Volume}}{\text{Rate}}
\\
&= \frac{1571.43}{25}
\\
&= 62.8572
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
t \approx 63 \text{ minutes}
$$

Hence,

$$
\boxed{\text{Time taken to empty the container}=63 \text{ minutes}}
$$
