# Exercise 7.4

## Question 1:

An aluminium sphere of radius 12 cm is melted to make a cylinder of radius 8 cm. Find the height of the cylinder.

---

## Solution:

Given,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Radius of the sphere }(R)
&= 12 \text{ cm}
\\
\text{Radius of the cylinder }(r)
&= 8 \text{ cm}
\end{aligned}
$$

Since the sphere is melted and recast into a cylinder,

$$
\text{Volume of sphere}
=
\text{Volume of cylinder}
$$

Volume of the sphere,

$$
\begin{aligned}
V
&=
\frac{4}{3}\pi R^3
\\
&=
\frac{4}{3}\pi(12)^3
\\
&=
2304\pi
\end{aligned}
$$

Volume of the cylinder,

$$
\begin{aligned}
V
&=
\pi r^2 h
\\
&=
\pi(8)^2h
\\
&=
64\pi h
\end{aligned}
$$

Equating the volumes,

$$
\begin{aligned}
2304\pi
&=
64\pi h
\\
2304
&=
64h
\\
h
&=
\frac{2304}{64}
\\
&=
36
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore,

$$
\boxed{\text{Height of the cylinder}=36 \text{ cm}}
$$
