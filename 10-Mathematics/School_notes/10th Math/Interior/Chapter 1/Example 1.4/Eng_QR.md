# Example 1.4

## Question:

Let

```text
A = {3,4,7,8}

B = {1,7,10}
```

Which of the following sets are relations from **A** to **B**?

```text
(i) R₁ = {(3,7), (4,7), (7,10), (8,1)}

(ii) R₂ = {(3,1), (4,12)}

(iii) R₃ = {(3,7), (4,10), (7,7), (7,8), (8,11), (8,7), (8,10)}
```

---


## Solution:

Given,

```text
A = {3,4,7,8}

B = {1,7,10}
```

```text
A × B

= {(3,1), (3,7), (3,10),
   (4,1), (4,7), (4,10),
   (7,1), (7,7), (7,10),
   (8,1), (8,7), (8,10)}
```

---

#### (i) Check R₁

```text
R₁ = {(3,7), (4,7), (7,10), (8,1)}
```

We note that,

```text
R₁ ⊆ A × B
```

Therefore,

```text
R₁ is a relation from A to B.
```

---

#### (ii) Check R₂

```text
R₂ = {(3,1), (4,12)}
```

Here,

```text
(4,12) ∈ R₂
```

but

```text
(4,12) ∉ A × B
```

Therefore,

```text
R₂ is not a relation from A to B.
```

---

#### (iii) Check R₃

```text
R₃ = {(3,7), (4,10), (7,7), (7,8), (8,11), (8,7), (8,10)}
```

Here,

```text
(7,8) ∈ R₃
```

but

```text
(7,8) ∉ A × B
```

Therefore,

```text
R₃ is not a relation from A to B.
```

---

## Final Answer:

```text
(i) R₁ is a relation from A to B.

(ii) R₂ is not a relation from A to B.

(iii) R₃ is not a relation from A to B.
```
