# Dihybrid Cross

A cross in which the inheritance of **two pairs of contrasting characters** is studied is called a **Dihybrid Cross**.

## Simple Explanation

- **"Di"** = Two  
- **"Hybrid"** = Cross breed(offspring resulting from a cross)

👉 A **dihybrid cross** studies **two traits at the same time**.

## Example

Consider two traits:
- **Seed shape**: Round (R) vs Wrinkled (r)
- **Seed color**: Yellow (Y) vs Green (y)

A cross like:

RrYy × RrYy

This is a **dihybrid cross**.

## Key Point

This type of cross helped **Gregor Mendel** discover the **Law of Independent Assortment**, which states that:

> Traits are inherited independently of each other.
